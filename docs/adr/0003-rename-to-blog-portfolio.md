# Repo renamed from Blog.Api to Blog.Portfolio

The repo began as a single blog's backend but is being restructured into a multi-app portfolio incubator for sixsideddice.com; "Blog.Api" no longer describes what it contains, and may never include a blog app at all. Renamed the repo, solution, and root namespace to `Blog.Portfolio` — kept "Blog" rather than naming after the `sixsideddice.com` brand, since the site's name or domain may change over time and the repo identity shouldn't be coupled to it.
